Heritage Site - Nanded University

SRTMU Nanded is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and is accredited with a B++ Grade by NAAC and approved by UGC. SRTMU Nanded comprises 14 schools of study on campus, 4 schools of study on sub-campus, and over 300 affiliated colleges. 63 lakhs and 4000 plus students.

History of University

Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University is established on 17th September, 1994. Nanded, Latur, Parbhani and Hingoli districts are came within the scope of this University. Asia's Biggest Lift water irrigation project is built on Godavari river.
